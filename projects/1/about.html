It's time to stop

As we all understand, Junkrat players simply don't have common sense to play other characters that can do much more like Pharah for example. But the underlying question remains, how do you convince a Junkrat player to switch? You can't. They are beyond reason and it's a lost cause. Simply report them and join our movement to disable Junkrat in competitive play.

Don't be fooled

Just because a Junkrat is top damage, does not mean he's good. It just means one of two things. Either the entire team is bad or Junkrat RNG'd hard enough to accidentally kill some people. It is typically the latter. Rarely are there players that are worse than the Junkrat main.
